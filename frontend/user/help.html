ಸಾಮಾನ್ಯ ಮತ್ತು ಮುಖಪುಟ | General & Home

ಏನು ಇದು? | What is JanaSeva portal?
JanaSeva ಪ್ರಮುಖ ಸರ್ಕಾರಿ ದಾಖಲೆ/ಸೇವೆಗಳ ಮಾಹಿತಿಗೆ ಮತ್ತು ಅರ್ಜಿಗೆ ಒಂದೇ ವೇದಿಕೆ; ಇಲ್ಲಿ ಇತಿಹಾಸ ಮತ್ತು ಅಧಿಕೃತ ಲಿಂಕ್‌ಗಳನ್ನೂ ನೋಡಬಹುದು | JanaSeva is a single window to access information and apply for key government documents and services, with application history and official links.
ಮುಖಪುಟವನ್ನು ಹೇಗೆ ಬಳಸುವುದು? | How do I navigate the home page?
ಮೇಲಿನ ನಾವಿಗೇಷನ್‌ನಲ್ಲಿ Home, History, Help ಬಳಸಿ. ಹುಡುಕಾಟದ ಮೂಲಕ ಸೇವೆ ಹುಡುಕಿ, Quick Access ಮೂಲಕ History/Services/Help/Official Websites ತೆರೆಯಬಹುದು | Use top navigation and search to find services, and Quick Access cards for History, Services, Help, and Official Websites.
ಅಧಿಕೃತ ಸರ್ಕಾರಿ ವೆಬ್‌ಸೈಟ್‌ಗಳು ಎಲ್ಲಿವೆ? | Where can I find official government websites?
ಮುಖಪುಟದ "Official Government Websites" ವಿಭಾಗದಲ್ಲಿ ಅಥವಾ Quick Access ನ "Official Websites" ಕಾರ್ಡ್‌ನಲ್ಲಿ UIDAI, PAN, Passport Seva, NVSP, Parivahan, NFSA ಮತ್ತು Nadakacheri ಲಿಂಕ್‌ಗಳು ಲಭ್ಯ | You can find UIDAI, PAN, Passport Seva, NVSP, Parivahan, NFSA and Nadakacheri links in the Official Websites section.

ಅರ್ಜಿಗಳ ಇತಿಹಾಸ | Application History

Where can I see my submitted applications?
Click "History" in the navigation or the "Application History" Quick Access card. You will see a table of all applications with Application Number, Applicant Name, Application Type, Date, and Status.
How do I search within my applications?
On the History page, use the search box to filter by application number, applicant name, application type, date, or status. The table updates as you type.
Why don't I see any applications?
Applications appear in History only after you submit a form (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Voter ID, Birth Certificate, Driving Licence, Ration Card, or Caste/Income). Data is stored in your browser (localStorage). If you clear site data, history will be lost.

ಸೇವೆಗಳು (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport ಇತ್ಯಾದಿ) | Services (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, etc.)

How do I apply for Aadhaar?
Go to Home -> Government Services -> Aadhaar Card -> Open. Read the About page, then click "Apply for Aadhaar." Fill the form with personal details, address, and upload Proof of Identity, Proof of Address, Proof of DOB, and photograph. Submit to get an application number. Biometrics are taken at an enrolment center in person.
What documents do I need for PAN card?
You need Proof of Identity (Aadhaar/Voter ID/Passport etc.), Proof of Address, Proof of Date of Birth, passport-size photo, and signature. Father's name and Aadhaar (for OTP) are mandatory. Upload scanned copies as per the form.
How do I apply for a Passport?
Open the Passport service from the home page. On the About page click "Apply for Passport." Fill in personal details, address, and upload identity proof, DOB proof, address proof, photograph, and signature. Submit to get an application number. Further steps may require appointment on the official Passport Seva portal.
How do I register for Voter ID?
Open Voter ID from Government Services -> Open. Click Apply and fill Form 6 with your name, father's name, DOB, gender, address, and upload identity proof, age proof, address proof, and photograph. Submit to receive an application number.
ಏನು ಇದು? | What is needed for a Birth Certificate?
You need the child's name, DOB, gender, place of birth, parents' names, and your contact (mobile & email). Upload proof of birth (hospital record/certificate), parents' identity proof, and address proof. Marriage certificate is optional.
What are the requirements for Driving Licence?
You need personal details (name, father's name, DOB, gender, address, email), licence type, Learner's Licence details, and uploads: identity proof, address proof, age proof, photograph, and medical certificate (Form 1A) if applicable.
How do I apply for a Ration Card?
Open Ration Card from the services. Fill in head of family name, phone, email, address, number of members, and Aadhaar numbers. Upload identity proof, address proof, income certificate, photographs, and bank passbook. Optional: surrender certificate, caste certificate, disability certificate.
How do I get Caste or Income Certificate?
Open "Caste & Income Certificate" from the services. Choose certificate type: Caste only, Income only, or Combined. Fill applicant details, address, and for caste upload caste proof; for income fill income details and upload affidavit. Upload identity, address, DOB proof and photograph, then submit.
Where do I get my application number after submitting?
Right after you submit any application form, you are taken to a success page that shows your Application Number. Save it for reference. The same application also appears under History so you can find it later by searching.

  3. Enter ಒತ್ತಿ ಅಥವಾ "Search" ಕ್ಲಿಕ್ ಮಾಡಿ | Press Enter or click the "Search" button.
  4. ಸಂಬಂಧಿತ About ಪುಟ ತೆರೆಯುತ್ತದೆ; ಅಲ್ಲಿಂದ "Apply" ಒತ್ತಿ | You will be taken to the service About page; click "Apply".

  • ಪಾಸ್‌ಪೋರ್ಟ್ | Passport: ಪಾಸ್‌ಪೋರ್ಟ್ ತೆರೆಯಿರಿ | Open Passport - ವೈಯಕ್ತಿಕ ವಿವರಗಳು ಮತ್ತು ಅಗತ್ಯ ದಾಖಲೆಗಳನ್ನು ಅಪ್‌ಲೋಡ್ ಮಾಡಿ | Fill details and upload required documents.

  • ರೇಷನ್ ಕಾರ್ಡ್ | Ration Card: ರೇಷನ್ ಕಾರ್ಡ್ ತೆರೆಯಿರಿ | Open Ration Card - ಕುಟುಂಬ ವಿವರಗಳು ಮತ್ತು ಅಗತ್ಯ ಪುರಾವೆಗಳನ್ನು ಸಲ್ಲಿಸಿ | Submit household details and required proofs.
